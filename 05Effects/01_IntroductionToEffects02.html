Showing and Hiding Content with animation effects.

jQuery can also show or hide content by means of animation effects. You can tell .show() and .hide() to use animation in a couple of ways. One is to pass in an argument of 'slow', 'normal', or 'fast', If you prefer more direct control over the duration of the animation effect, you can pass the desired duration in milliseconds to .show() and .hide()
